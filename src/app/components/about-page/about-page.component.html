ESG Responsibilities

Environmental

Calorie Extraction is committed to reducing its environmental impact. We strive to reduce our emissions wherever possible. We reduce, reuse, and recycle from start to finish and incorporate that mindset from start to finish. We also encourage patrons to reduce their carbon footprint by walking, which is a good form of exercise, as much as possible, to get the best of both worlds.

Social

We are committed to a diverse and inclusive culture where everyone's voice is heard. Calorie Extraction makes sure everyone feels like they belong and are empowered to put their very best foot forward. We support local communities, the development and well-being of our employees, and education.

Governance

Calorie Extraction puts itself to the highest standards of social responsibility and accountability in operations and reporting. We collect data and ensure it is being used responsibly to the highest degree. We make sure all data is presented accurately and not at a questionable angle. We comply with all rules and regulations set by lawmakers.
